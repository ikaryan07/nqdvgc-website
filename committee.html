Governance

How We Operate

Incorporated Association

Registered under Queensland law with a formal legal structure for our operations.

Annual General Meeting

Held annually where members elect the committee, review financials, and vote on key decisions.

Not-for-Profit

All club revenue is reinvested into member activities, veteran welfare, and community engagement.
