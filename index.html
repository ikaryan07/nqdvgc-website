Who We Are

Veteran Mateship Through Golf

We're a not-for-profit club based in Townsville for current and former ADF members and their families. We play social and competition golf — all skill levels, all branches of service.

It's about mateship, wellbeing, and good times on the course. Whether you're a scratch golfer or picking up a club for the first time, you belong here.
